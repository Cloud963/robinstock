RobinStock is a decentralized trading platform that brings tokenized real-world stocks to the blockchain. By combining traditional financial markets with DeFi infrastructure, we're creating a bridge between two worlds that enables:

- **24/7 Trading**: Trade tokenized stocks anytime, anywhere
- **Fractional Ownership**: Own fractions of expensive stocks
- **DeFi Integration**: Use stocks as collateral, provide liquidity, earn yields
- **Transparency**: All transactions on-chain and verifiable
- **Accessibility**: No minimum investment, no geographic restrictions

## üåü What is RobinStock?

RobinStock is an Automated Market Maker (AMM) DEX specifically designed for tokenized stocks. It allows users to:

1. **Swap** tokenized stocks instantly using an AMM model
2. **Provide Liquidity** and earn trading fees
3. **Stake LP Tokens** for additional RBS token rewards
4. **Trade 24/7** without traditional market hours

## üîë Key Features

### Automated Market Maker (AMM)
- Constant product formula (x * y = k)
- 0.3% trading fee distributed to liquidity providers
- No order books, instant swaps
- Slippage protection

### Tokenized Stocks
- ERC20 tokens representing real-world stocks (TSLA, AMZN, AAPL, etc.)
- Each token backed by real stock value
- Oracle-based price feeds
- Transparent and auditable

### Liquidity Provision
- Earn 0.3% of all trades in your pool
- Receive LP tokens representing your share
- Add/remove liquidity anytime
- Stake LP tokens for additional rewards

### Staking Rewards
- Stake LP tokens to earn RBS governance tokens
- Variable APR based on pool performance
- Claim rewards anytime
- No lock-up periods

## üåç Real World Assets (RWA)

RobinStock is part of the growing RWA (Real World Assets) category in DeFi. RWAs are blockchain tokens that represent ownership of real-world assets like:

- Stocks and equities
- Real estate
- Commodities
- Bonds
- Art and collectibles

### Why RWAs Matter

1. **Liquidity**: Bring liquidity to traditionally illiquid assets
2. **Accessibility**: Enable global access to local markets
3. **Transparency**: On-chain verification and tracking
4. **Composability**: Use in DeFi protocols
5. **Fractional Ownership**: Lower barriers to entry
